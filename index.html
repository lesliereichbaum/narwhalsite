Are Narwhals Real?

Despite the fact that they are often called "The Unicorns of the Sea", these mythical-looking creatures are in fact real. Narwhals are a type of whale that possess a "tusk" protruting from the top of its body. Thye live year-round in the Arctic around Canada, Greenland and, Russia. Narwhals are piscivores and mostly eat halibut, cod, squid and shrimp. Narwhals can live to up to 50 years old. Today, Narwhals are threatened by climate change and oil and gas development.
